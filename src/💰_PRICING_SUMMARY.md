## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades are immediate, downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
We'll notify you when you're approaching limits. To add more users/branches, simply upgrade to the next plan.

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees! You only pay for your chosen subscription plan.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime. No cancellation fees. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

### Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis within the first 14 days. Contact support@ShopSpot.com.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and USSD through Paystack.

### Is my data secure?
Yes! We use enterprise-grade encryption and security. All data is stored securely on Supabase with daily backups.
