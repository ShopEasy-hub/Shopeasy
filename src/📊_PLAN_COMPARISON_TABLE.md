## ❓ Common Questions

### Q: What happens if I don't subscribe after trial?
**A:** Access is completely blocked. Data is preserved for 30 days. Subscribe anytime to restore access.

### Q: Can I upgrade mid-month?
**A:** Yes! You pay pro-rated difference. Extra features activate immediately.

### Q: Can I downgrade?
**A:** Yes, but extra entities become view-only until you delete them or upgrade again.

### Q: What if I hit my user limit?
**A:** You cannot add more users. Existing users continue working. Upgrade to add more.

### Q: Is my data safe if I downgrade?
**A:** YES! We NEVER delete your data. Extra items are just marked view-only.

### Q: Can I switch between plans freely?
**A:** Yes! Upgrade/downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately.
